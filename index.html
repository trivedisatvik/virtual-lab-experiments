WELCOME TO WEB TECHNOLOGY VIRTUAL LAB

You probably know that computers don't communicate with each other the way that people do. Instead, computers require codes, or directions. These binary codes and commands allow computers to process needed information. Every second, billions upon billions of ones and zeros are processed in order to provide you with the information you need. So what does that have to do with your ability to post your latest pictures online? Everything. The methods by which computers communicate with each other through the use of markup languages and multimedia packages is known as web technology. In the past few decades, web technology has undergone a dramatic transition, from a few marked up web pages to the ability to do very specific work on a network without interruption.

Why is it important?

As individuals, we highly esteem our amazing adaptation skills and capacity to change with the occasions. Coming from this, when the social world moved from relational associations with web associations, so did the universe of business. Web development is the establishment of 90% organisations. For any business, a website matters more than anything, with respect to reaching customers on the web. For the growth of your business, your voice should be heard, your image should be seen, and your objectives should be achieved. Website development is the way to cause those things to occur. A website is the representation of the business on the web. These days, each business comprehends the necessity for having a web page and are setting in endeavouring to structure and develop the best page for taking their things or services on the web. There is no substitute in being clear to customers.
